    As Chinese born Berliner I have had many sichuan noodles in Berlin, China and the US. This one was ok. The noodle were too soft and overcooked. I usually like them al dente and a bit more firm. The taste was ok, it obviously has the sichuan spiciness but was not something I'd come back for. The portions are good if you are looking to receive a large portions of noodles. The amount of protein was mediocre given the price we paid.

    We went in and asked for cold noodles and just received a "no" from the waitress. The politeness of this place is classy Chinese, home sweet home, if you want to be authentic gotta make sure the service is also authentically bad :D

    Why should you come?
    If you love noodles and the sichuan spiciness, check out this place. If you have never tried this type of taste, check out this place. If you love authentic Chinese food in general and want to find THE place that reminds you of China, skip this place. What I can tell though is that this place is definitely better than the other sichuan noodle spot in Kreuzberg
