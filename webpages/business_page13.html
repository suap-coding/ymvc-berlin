    You can smell this place from a few blocks away, it smells and tastes like the pizza in Rome. \xc2\xa0Affordable, small, and quaint, they also do fresh pasta dishes. \xc2\xa0I had a pizza topped with prosciutto and arugula and bought a beer next door (it's cheaper). The supermarket next door is affiliated with the pizzeria, (tip given to me by the Italian born employee).

    I loved how there were candlesticks on each table, created a cozy atmosphere for a cold Berlin day.

    This pizza was absolutely delicious, atmosphere cozy and quaint, and the two gentleman working the counter were friendly and helpful. Cheap lunch/dinner and they are opened late. Wouldn't change a thing!
